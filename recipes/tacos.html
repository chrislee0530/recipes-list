Beef Tacos

Description

These classic beef tacos are quick, easy, and loaded with flavor. Perfect for busy weeknights or fun gatherings with friends and family.

Customize them with your favourite toppings like cheese, salsa, and sour cream â€” they never disappoint!

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning (or homemade)
  • 8 small taco shells
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Grated cheese
  • Sour cream (optional)
  • Salsa (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook onion and garlic in a pan until soft.
  2. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in taco seasoning and a splash of water. Simmer 5 mins.
  4. Warm taco shells in oven or microwave.
  5. Spoon beef into shells and add toppings.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
