Chocolate Chip Cookies

Descriptions

These chocolate chip cookies are soft, chewy, and packed with melty chocolate. They’re the perfect treat for any occasion — or no occasion at all.

Bake a batch in under 30 minutes and enjoy them fresh out of the oven with a glass of milk.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together butter and sugars.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla. Mix well.
  4. Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet.
  7. Bake 10–12 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Let cool slightly before serving.
