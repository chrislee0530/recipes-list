Classic Lasagna

Description

This hearty and comforting lasagna recipe is the ultimate Italian-American classic. Layered with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese, it's a perfect weekend or holiday dish that brings everyone together.

Whether you’re cooking for a family dinner or meal prepping for the week, this lasagna is satisfying, flavorful, and sure to impress. It’s warm, cheesy, and completely irresistible.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles. Drain and set aside.
  3. Sauté onion and garlic. Add ground beef and cook until brown.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and seasonings. Simmer 15 mins.
  5. Mix ricotta, egg, and half the Parmesan in a bowl.
  6. Layer sauce, noodles, ricotta mix, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  7. Repeat layers. Top with sauce, mozzarella, and remaining Parmesan.
  8. Cover with foil. Bake 25 mins. Remove foil and bake 15 more mins.
  9. Let it cool for 10 mins. Serve and enjoy!
